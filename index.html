About Us

"BKC Boyzs" is a vibrant group brimming with witty banter, dark humor, and spirited discussions on Hindu nationalist politics. As a collective of like-minded individuals, they revel in the art of 'bakchodi' and share a penchant for dark memes that tickle the edges of propriety. Led by members such as abhrajit, Raj, Ankit, Nil, Argha, Rupam, Ronit, Sayan, Vipoo, Parthiv, Uday, Arijit, and Arkay. Within the confines of this group, laughter reigns supreme, serving as a sanctuary for those seeking respite from the mundane and a platform for unabashed expression. Welcome to the world of BKC Boyzs, where wit meets wisdom, and no topic is off-limits.
